Use of Internet Technologies and Its impact in our daily life

INTERNET TECHNOLOGY Technology is defined as the scientific knowledge of using tools and techniques that makes our work easier and faster. It also refers to the devices or machines that we use in our daily life. Technology has made our world much more easier that we could imagine it to be. It is one of the basic need of people in today’s world. The importance of it is increasing day by day in the world. Technology is categorized in various types like internet technology, education technology, health technology , industry technology and so on. Among this, internet technology is one of the widespread technology in this modern era. Internet technology is a technical field that covers the necessary skills to develop any kind of applications on the internet. It makes our life easy, simple and fast. This technology has become of the essential factor in the life of every people. Internet technology helps us with facts and figures and gives people knowledge about social, political, economic process of development.

In today’s world, internet technology has a great impact in our life. It is internet due to which our lives have become easier. With the help of it , we can share our thoughts to a large number of people across globe. We can also communicate with the people globally. This technology has made us easier to communicate with our relatives who are living far away from us. Likewise, we can get the information on various topics with the help of internet. We can get the information of various incidents that are taking place in different parts of the world with the help of internet. This technology has brought changes in the field of education too. With the help of this technology the education is no longer boring instead it is interesting to the students. The online classes held during the time of COVID-19 shows the importance of internet technology in student’s life. Similarly, internet technology is used in banking too. People can view their accounts, check their bank statements and can transfer money by using it. This technology is also used to advertise different products to grab the attention of people towards their product. Similarly various social medias like Facebook, YouTube has become a good platform for people to fulfill their needs and to follow their passion.

Internet technology has both positive and negative impacts in our life. Internet technology helps to stay connected with the people all over the globe. With the help of internet, email and other various types of social media people can communicate with each other easily. It also provides job opportunities globally. We can also apply for the jobs in foreign countries easily with the help of internet. Internet technology has made people aware about their rights and duties towards their country. With the help of it we can get information of different places easily. We can share our ideas and thoughts to people using internet. It has played a great role in education sector. Internet technology has helped students to study in more understandable way by learning and watching at the same time and also students can study watching videos in YouTube. Internet technology has various negative effects in our life. People become quite lazy as their work is easily replaced by the machines. People are much more dependent upon the technologies which is not good. The excessive use of internet creates serious mental problem. People use mobile phones even when they drive which might lead to road accidents. The personal data is used for many proposes and we might not know about it when visiting different sites. Some people even get involved in cyber bullying and they cause troubles for people. With the advancement of internet technology people are forgetting their culture and adopting the culture of foreign countries which is not good. Many people get addicted to the internet which may lead to serious health issues. Likewise, people steal various personal data and misuse it using internet technology.

In conclusion, internet technology has completed changed our lives. Its need is increasing day by day un the world. The internet is a powerful media to share our thoughts and ideas. It has become a great platform for many people too. Internet technology has brought changes in personal as well as professional life. Every people should using internet technologies wisely so that it might not cause any negative impact to the people. With the help of internet technologies people of different worlds are connected to each other. The internet is a profitable tool if it is used correctly.
